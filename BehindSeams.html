Behind The Seams:

First Costume Backstory

The Green Curtain Dress

My first big costume project was nothing short of an adventure—a labor of love inspired by Scarlet O'Hara's unforgettable green curtain dress. It all began with a princess costume pattern from Joann Fabrics that I transformed to mirror the elegance of Scarlet’s design. The real magic, however, was in the details. I found budget-friendly curtain tassels at my local fabric store that perfectly captured the character’s charm, tying the ensemble together seamlessly.

One of my favorite parts of the process was crafting the velvet bonnet adorned with feathers. I meticulously referenced costume photos to stay true to the original design and ensure authenticity. It wasn’t a quick project—day by day, little by little, it came together, evolving into a masterpiece that I could truly be proud of.

This experience proved to me that with passion, creativity, and determination, anything is possible. Whether you're dreaming of your own costume creation or simply want to embrace your inner designer, I hope my journey inspires you to bring your imagination to life.

A Note on Costume Selection

My passion for sewing is centered on celebrating the artistry and craftsmanship behind iconic costumes. While some designs, like Scarlett O’Hara’s green curtain dress, are tied to historical contexts, my focus is purely on honoring the beauty of the costume itself. I approach each creation with care and respect, always striving to bring characters to life through the magic of design and creativity.
